       About this lealfet
       Although most people have no problems when using health or social 
       care services, sometimes things can go wrong. 
       This lealfet explains what to do if you, a member of your family or 
       someone acting on your behalf wants to complain about care or 
       treatment that you have received.
       For many, the thought of making a complaint can be daunting, but 
       care services should welcome the opportunity to respond to your 
       concerns and try to put things right.
