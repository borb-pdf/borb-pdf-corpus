                    •   When and how do I take this medicine?                                                    
                    Always take this medicine as your doctor has told you. If you do not take                    
                    rivaroxaban regularly it will not be effective and your risk of a clot forming will 
                    increase.                                                                                    
                                                                                                                 
                   The dose is usually 20mg once a day. This dose may be reduced to                              
                   15mg once a day if you have kidney problems, are taking other medicines                       
                   which interact or have a higher risk of bleeding. Your doctor will discuss this               
                   with you.
