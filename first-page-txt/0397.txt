                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Dive In!
                                   Book Review Questions for Use with any Novel
                                                    Write a Review of the novel you have studied under the following headings. Use the 
                                                    questions to help you to plan. Decide which questions suit your novel.
                                                    STORYLINE                                      Ì        Did the story have a good beginning?
                                                                                                   Ì        Was this story believable?
                                                                                                   Ì        Was there a lot of action? 
                                                                                                   Ì        What was the most exciting moment in the book?
                                                                                                   Ì         What did you think was the most important point, or climax, of the 
                                                                                                            novel?
                                                                                                   Ì        Did you want to read the book to the end? Why?
                                                                                                   Ì        Were you happy with the ending?
                                                     Use examples and quotes from the novel to make points when answering these questions.
                                                    SETTING                                        Ì    
                                                                                                            When and where is your novel set?
                                                                                                   Ì        Was there anything unusual or different about it?
                                                                                                   Ì        Were the descriptions good?
                                                                                                   Ì        Could you see the places in your mind?
                                                    Remember to give examples!
                                                    CHARACTERS  Ì    Pick the main character in the novel you studied.
                                                                                                   Ì        Were they ‘real’?
                                                                                                   Ì        Did you like them? Why or why not?
                                                                                                   Ì         If you were describing them to a stranger, what words would you 
                                                                                                            use?
                                                                                                   Ì        If you could ask them any questions, what would you ask?
                                                                                                   Ì        What other people were signiifcant in their lives, and why?
                                                    Did you give examples?
